Along the way we will see buffalo and maybe even more nature if you Arriving Mae Ma Nai Karen Village, where we will stay the night in a homestay and your guide will cook you a delicious dinner which you can help prepare if you want. This is a small village with about 20 houses. You will get to see a very rural way of life and how the locals live in the rainforest. The village is at an altitude of about 1,400 meters above sea level. You can walk around the village and enjoy the evening scene of the surrounding mountains. Among other things you can see the top of Thailands 3rd highest mountain, Doi Chiang Dao. - item: >- Day 2: Wake up and have breakfast. After breakfast we will be leaving the Karen village and hike for approximately 1 to 2 hours to the Mae Tang River, here we will prepare our bamboo rafts on which we will raft down the river for approximately 2 to 3 hours. Each bamboo raft has a rack in the middle for luggage which keeps it dry. You will enjoy the scenic views while we raft down the river and enjoy the nature from all around you. If you like the guide can show you how to steer the raft. (It is not so easy) - item: >- We will finally reach Sopkhai village, you can refresh yourself and will have lunch and you can wander around the small village and across a long wooden hanging bridge and we will then head back to Chiang Mai, arriving in the city around 16.30 to 17.30 notes: >- Trails may very during different seasons and be slippery in rainy season.
