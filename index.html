Trek Thailand

Eco Tours and Treks in Northern Thailand

Welcome to your next adventure! Ethnic Hilltribe Eco Trails is a small, family-run tour operator offering eco tours and treks around Chiang Mai, Thailand. We take travelers on authentic and breath-taking journeys through the unspoiled jungles and hills of Pha Daeng in Chiang Dao, Doi Inthanon in Chiang Mai and more.

We don't do your typical hikes, instead, we take you on a Karen hilltribe style jungle walk, creating your own path, immersing yourself in the forest, and getting to know the local culture. If you're looking to get away from the tourist experience and explore nature like a true native, then you've come to the right place....
 

Family Run

We're a husband and wife team working with a close network of amazing guides. When you're with us, you're family, and we'll make sure you're well cared for. We also partner with other trusted family-run tour operators to offer further trips to enhance your experience.

Off the Beaten Path

You won't see our trails advertized anywhere else, because we're the only ones who trek our one-of-a-kind special routes. Our guides grew up in these hills and know the jungle like no other, we can show you the side of northern Thailand only a lucky few ever see.

Sustainable

We are passionate about the environment, and have deep roots in the communities we work in. Not only do we strive for the most environmentally sustainable business practices, we also make sure our work directly benefits our community partners....

Featured Tours and Treks

We're best known for our completely off-the-beaten-track hiking trails in some of northern Thailand's most beautiful National Parks. Journey with us to Doi Inthanon National Park and climb the highest mountain in Thailand or to Pha Daeng National Park in Chiang Dao, rich in tropical flora and fauna.

The places we go are incredibly scenic and non-touristic. Trek through the heart of the rainforest, visit hidden waterfalls, discover incredible view points or catch a glimpse of wild gibbons. Visit hilltribe villages where our guides are from, and discover the depths of their ethnic wisdom. Learn about their way of life and the secrets of traditional herbal medicines used for centuries.

Our two day trails will either take you to sleep in a hilltribe village in the mountains of Chiang Dao or to the Churkee Farmstay over looking the mountains or homestay amid the rice fields of a Karen hilltribe village in Doi Inthanon National Park.
