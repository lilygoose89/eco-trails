Frequently Asked Questions

I am a solo traveller, can I join a group?

Of course, if we have a group scheduled for one of our trails, you can join this trip no problem.

How big are your groups?

Depending on the trail, we take can take a maximum of 11 people but our groups average between 5 and 9 people.

Do you offer private tours?

Yes we do, contact us directly to discuss.

Can you customize/personalize or combine tours?

Yes, we can for private tours, though there are some constraints depending on the area and what is desired. We will always try our best to personalize for peoples needs.

Do you also arrange other tours? (such as ATV'ing, mountain biking, white-water rafting, elephants etc)

We can arrange just about anything for you by partnering with other companies. We aim to partner with other family run businesses like ours with ethical practices and strong reputations whenever possible.

Can the weather conditions effect the trails?

Yes, in rainy season it can make the trails slippery and sometimes we have to take another route if it is not possible to trek the usual way.

Where do we sleep and what can we expect?

Depending on which trail you are doing, we have different types of sleeping arrangements. On some of our trips you sleep in farmstays or homestays (2 Day Doi Inthanon) which are part of a social initiative to help local communities make an income in their villages from visitors. You sleep in cute bamboo huts overlooking the mountains and rice fields built by the local Karen Family or in a home of a local karen hilltribe family. The accommodations are rustic and simple, the way locals live. Please go in with an open mind and heart.
Our 2 Day Chiang Dao you will stay in a treehouse homestay or a homestay in Mae Mae village, these accommodations are modest but more comfortable, this is good for people looking for a little comfort to sleep after a trek.

Is bedding provided?

Yes, we provide bedding on all overnight trips including basic floor mattress, blanket, pillows and mosquito net.

Can I store my luggage with you?

Yes, we can keep your luggage at our office until you return.

Can you recommend a guesthouse or hotel?

Yes, you can stay at our lovely guesthouse called Luna Guesthouse 2, Chiang Mai. You can check us out on Facebook here. If you are looking for a more luxurious accommodation, contact us directly for recommendations.

I am looking for a real hiking, authentic and non touristic experience, do you offer this?

Yes, you have come to the right place! All our trips are specialized and completely off the beaten track. Our trails range from easy to hard depending on which one you choose.

We are a family with young children are any treks suitable for us?

Yes we have several treks and soft-adventures which are great for families. Check here for family-friendly treks.

What should we wear?

You should wear comfortable cool clothing to hike in hot season, shoes with good grip, hiking boots are best, sports shoes are fine if they have good grip. In rainy season we recommend, longer pants to hike in and to always take a rain coat with. T-shirts and shorts or T-shirts and long pants.
