About Ethnic Hilltribe Eco Trails

We are a family-run tour operator specializing in off-the-beaten-path, Karen hilltribe style nature hikes in the scenic hills of northern Thailand. Our offices are based in Chiang Mai, Thailand, and we look forward to taking you on your next adventure.

From our family to yours, join us on an incredible adventure.

Ethnic Hilltribe Eco Trails was founded by Khun Mit and his wife Kischa. They started these trails with the dream to give people a chance to experience real authentic hiking trails, visit hilltribe villages and experience the culture of northern Thailand hilltribes in the same way they enjoy so much.
